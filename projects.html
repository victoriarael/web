Online Bookstore for Growth

This is not just a bookstore — it's a curated space filled with books that nourish the mind and soul. Built with HTML and CSS, this project blends functionality with purpose. It showcases titles that promote personal development, mental wellness, emotional intelligence, and self-discovery.

The platform is clean, responsive, and beginner-friendly — with room to grow into a full e-commerce system. It's my way of using tech to share what has helped me become more grounded, hopeful, and inspired.
