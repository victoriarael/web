My Hobbies

I really treasure my personal time — those quiet moments to relax, reflect, and just unwind from the busyness of life. To make the most out of this precious time, I always try to accompany it with something that brings me joy, peace, or inspiration.

Here are some of the things I enjoy doing in my free time:

  • Reading: I love getting lost in a good book — whether it's a novel, self-help, or something educational. Reading helps me escape, learn, and grow.
  • Listening to Music: Music is my therapy. I enjoy different genres depending on my mood.
  • Nature Walks: Being in green spaces clears my mind and brings peace.
  • Watching Inspiring Content: Documentaries and podcasts are my go-to!
  • Coding for Fun: Exploring small creative coding challenges is relaxing and fun.

My hobbies help me recharge and stay connected to myself, which I believe is essential for personal growth and happiness.

Why Hobbies Matter

These hobbies are more than just activities — they’re part of how I take care of my mental health and creativity.

What we do with our free time shapes how we show up in everything else — especially in tech and life.
